What are Private Market PPOs?

Private Market PPOs (Private Placement Offerings) are investment opportunities in private companies that are not available on public stock exchanges. These offerings allow accredited investors to purchase equity in high-growth companies before they IPO, often at favorable valuations compared to eventual public market prices. Jack Estes DeBrabander specializes in sourcing, vetting, and structuring these exclusive private placement opportunities across multiple high-growth sectors.

What types of private placement offerings does Jack Estes DeBrabander offer?

Jack Estes DeBrabander offers three primary categories of private placement offerings: Pre-IPO Private Placements in late-stage companies with a clear path to going public, Growth Equity PPOs in high-growth technology, healthcare, and fintech companies that are rapidly scaling, and Secondary Market Shares providing access to established private company stock at negotiated valuations through our extensive network.

Who qualifies to invest in private market PPOs?

Private market PPOs offered through Jack Estes DeBrabander are available exclusively to accredited investors as defined under Regulation D of the Securities Act of 1933. Accredited investors typically include individuals with a net worth exceeding $1 million (excluding primary residence) or annual income exceeding $200,000 individually ($300,000 combined with spouse) for the past two consecutive years with a reasonable expectation of the same for the current year.
